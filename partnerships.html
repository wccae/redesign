Community Partnerships

Contra Costa County Adult Education Consortium

WCCAE is a member of the county adult education consortium. CCCAEC provides oversight and funding to adult education programs. The consortium helps plan, implement and grow programs for English language instruction, academic education services, career and technical education courses and programs for persons with intellectual disabilities.
