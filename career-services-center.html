Career Services Center

College and Career Readiness Services

The Career Services Center assists students with resources to help them achieve their career goal. Students at the Career Services Center receive support choosing a career by using assessment tools to examine their values, personality, interests, and abilities. Assessment tools include the initial interview. The results are used to help map out a career and/or a job search plan for the student to pursue.
